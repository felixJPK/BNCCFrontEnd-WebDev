Library Management System

A Library Management System (LMS) is a software application designed to streamline the management of library resources and services. It helps librarians efficiently catalog, track, and manage books, magazines, and other materials. With features for checking in and out, inventory management, and user account handling, an LMS enhances the efficiency of library operations and improves the overall user experience.

It aims to support users in making informed decisions about their health and wellness.
